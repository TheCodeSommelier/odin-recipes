Roasted Garlic Lemon Broccoli

Description

Roasted Garlic Lemon Broccoli is a healthy and delicious side dish that can be prepared quickly and easily. Fresh broccoli florets are roasted in the oven with garlic and lemon juice until they are tender and slightly charred. The garlic and lemon add a tangy, zesty flavor to the broccoli, making it a perfect accompaniment to any main dish. This dish is not only flavorful but also packed with nutrients, making it a great addition to any healthy diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads broccoli, separated into florets
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Toss broccoli florets with extra virgin olive oil, sea salt, pepper, and garlic in a large bowl. Spread the broccoli out in an even layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until florets are tender enough to pierce the stems with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove and transfer to a serving platter.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice liberally over broccoli before serving for a refreshing, tangy finish.
